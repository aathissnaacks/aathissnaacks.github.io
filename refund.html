Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellations are only accepted if requested immediately after placing the order.

Orders once processed or shipped cannot be cancelled.

Perishable items (snacks and edible products) are not eligible for cancellation.

Customers must report damaged or incorrect products within the same day of delivery.

Refunds will be processed within 1–2 working days after approval.
