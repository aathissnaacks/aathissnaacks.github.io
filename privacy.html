Privacy Policy

Last updated on 04-04-2026

Aathi Shivaya Group of Companies (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or place an order.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Order and transaction details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate order updates and customer support
  • To improve our services and customer experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Payment Information

All payments are securely processed through trusted third-party payment gateways such as Cashfree. We do not store your card details, UPI credentials, or banking information on our servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Your information may only be shared with:

  • Payment gateway providers (for transaction processing)
  • Delivery partners (for order fulfillment)
  • Government authorities if required by law

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve user experience. These cookies do not collect sensitive personal information.

7. User Rights

You may contact us to update or delete your personal information at any time.

8. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.
